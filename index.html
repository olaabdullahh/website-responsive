Popular destinations in Palestine

Gaza is a Palestinian coastal city, the largest city in the Gaza Strip and located in its north, at the southern end of the eastern coast of the Mediterranean Sea. It is 78 km to the south from the city of Jerusalem

Ramallah is located within the Jerusalem mountain range, overlooking the Palestinian coast of the Mediterranean Sea, which is about 45 kilometers to the west. Most of its residents today are Muslims, with a strong Christian minority.

Alkhalel City is considered one of the oldest inhabited historical cities in the world, dating back to more than 4000 years BC. The city of Hebron gained its fame after the arrival of our master Abraham, peace be upon him, where he died and was buried. The city has become known today as “Khalil al-Rahman,” named after the grandfather of the prophets, Abraham, peace be upon him.
